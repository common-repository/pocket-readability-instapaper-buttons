Display official Pocket Instapaper and Readability buttons to your posts/pages.

== Description ==

Make your users save time to read later your thrilling content by adding the official buttons from the most used apps around the world : Pocket, Instapaper, Readability and since v1.1 Kindle !
Choose which one you want to display, which size, when and where to display them (posts, articles, both, at the start/end/both of content).

Look at the changelog tab to see new features !

More features in progress...

== Installation ==

There are three ways to install this plugin.

1. Go to the *Plugins* menu and click *Add New*.
2. Search for *Pocket Readability Instapaper*.
3. Click **Install Now** next to the *Read later for Wordpress* plugin.
4. Activate the plugin.

Or you can also :

1. Go to [WordPress](http://wordpress.org/plugins "The best CMS ever")
2. Search for *Pocket Readability Instapaper*.
3. Click the *Pocket Readability Instapaper Buttons* link.
4. Click the *Download* button.
5. Unzip the .zip file and upload it on your FTP in the wp-content/plugins/ folder.
6. Activate the plugin through the Plugins menu in Wordpress Administration. 

Or you can also :

1. Go to [WordPress](http://wordpress.org/plugins "The best CMS ever")
2. Search for *Pocket Readability Instapaper*.
3. Click the *Pocket Readability Instapaper Buttons* link.
4. Click the *Download* button.
5. Go to the Plugins menu in Wordpress Administration.
6. Click *Add* button.
7. Click *Send* link.
8. Attach the .zip file you just downloaded.

Then, click *Read later for Wordpress* link in the *Settings* menu in Wordpress to set the plugin as you want.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does this plugin provide a shortcode to put manually everywhere we want ? =

Hopefully not ! If you posted hundreds of stuff on your blog, you won't have to edit every post ! The plugin automatically add buttons everywhere you want. Look at the plugin *Settings* panel in Wordpress Administration to set it.

== Why this plugin ==

Apps like Pocket, Readability & Instapaper are used by tens of millions around the world, especially to save long and captivating articles to read it later offline.
Usually, to save an article from a mobile device, you have to click three times. (The *Menu* button, then *Share* button, then *Add to Pocket*, or any app you use).
In the long run, it's painful for users. And if it's painful for users, it's not good for your blog.
